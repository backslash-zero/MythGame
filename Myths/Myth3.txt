In the parched village, frogs and snakes writhed under desperate heels, sacrifices to the altar of rain. Yet, the drought persisted, a mirage of despair. As the last creature fell, a shudder swept through the villagers. Skin turned slick, limbs shrank, and eyes bulged. They were now the vermin they'd slain. Abandoning their cursed home, they slithered and hopped from village to village, a grim procession seeking refuge in a world that once feared them.
