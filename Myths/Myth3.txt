Water surged, filling the hut, an illusion shimmering and strange. The inhabitants splashed, catching fish and fowl in the whirling chaos. Laughter echoed, then stilled as they noticed the frogs. Bulbous eyes watched, unblinking, as the catch wriggled, transforming into writhing serpents. Panic rose, the illusion dispelling, the dwelling dry once more, the frogs croaking out a mocking, ancient song.
