The crone, ancient and bent, raised her staff. With a cry, she struck the earth, and the hero ascended, spiraling into the heavens. The crone's lips moved in silent incantation, her hands tracing patterns in the air. Gently, the hero descended, feet touching the ground, eyes wide with wonder. She handed him a stone, its surface shimmering with worlds unseen. "Step through," she whispered, and he did, boundaries dissolving, worlds merging at his touch.
