A crone, eyes like embers, promised the hero eternal life, if he'd bend his virtue and serve her whim. Ensnared by her promise, he agreed. She morphed, her wrinkles smoothing, her staff becoming a tender hand. Beauty incarnate, she led him through trials, his conscience echoing each betrayal. Their journey ended at the underworld's maw. "Here, you live forever," she whispered, shoving him into the abyss, her laughter echoing as she transformed back into the crone.
