In the alley's shadows, the crone huddled, her promise a whisper on cold breath. "Eternal life, if you fetch me spices from the Asian market." The hero returned, arms laden with aromatic bundles. In a hidden kitchen, she taught him hotpot, her wrinkles smoothing as steam rose. Hand in hand, they entered a club, door locking behind them. In the strobe-lit dark, they danced, their eternity a pulsing beat, their love a sanctuary in the neon night.
