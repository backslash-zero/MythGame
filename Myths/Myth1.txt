In the crone's hut, a promise hung like smoke. "Eternal life, hero, if you defy your conscience for me." Her gnarled fingers touched his, and she shifted, ancient skin blooming into youth. Together, they traversed trials, her beauty his beacon, his convictions fraying. In the underworld's heart, she revealed their fate. "Here, we live forever." Her crystal ball reflected the world above, ignored by them both, their eternal gaze turned inward, kindred spirits in endless night.
