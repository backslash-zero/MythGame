The crone, eyes like embers, leaned close. "Eternal life," she whispered, "if you do my bidding, against your heart's cry." The hero hesitated, then nodded. She cackled, pressing a stone into his palm. "Step through," she urged, pointing to a shimmering boundary. He did, gasping as worlds shifted around him. With each border crossed, his convictions frayed, life stretching endless before him, a bittersweet promise kept.
