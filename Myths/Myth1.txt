The hero stood, arms outstretched, as the ancient crone raised her gnarled staff. With a cry, she struck the earth, and the hero was lifted, spiraling into the sky, swallowed by clouds. The crone whispered secrets to the wind, which carried the hero gently back to the ground. She pressed a stone into his palm, its surface swirling with worlds unseen. With her guidance, he stepped through borders once impervious, becoming a bridge between realms, a wanderer of worlds.
