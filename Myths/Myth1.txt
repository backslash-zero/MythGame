The crone, eyes aglow, promised, "Eternal life, if you do my bidding, against your heart's cry." The hero agreed, but as he stepped back, the crone contorted, becoming a beast, wild and fearsome. It led him through shadowed realms, testing his resolve at each border. Yet, with every challenge, his convictions burned brighter. Together, they crossed worlds, his spirit unyielding, his life eternal, his integrity unbroken.
